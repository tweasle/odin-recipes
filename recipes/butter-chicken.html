Description

These butter chicken breasts are dipped in beaten eggs and cracker crumbs, then baked with butter. These chicken breasts are really tender and moist with excellent flavor! I never have leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup crushed buttery round cracker crumbs
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  • Ground black pepper to taste
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1/2 cup butter, cut into pieces
