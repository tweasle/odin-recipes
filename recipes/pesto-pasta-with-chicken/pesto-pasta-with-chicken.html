Pesto Pasta with Chicken

Description

This chicken pesto pasta is easy and deli fontcious. Serve with crusty bread and salad for a quick dinner. Use as much or as little pesto sauce as you like. Using homemade pesto will taste even better, but it adds to prep time. Enjoy!

source

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 oz) package bow tie pasta
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 pinch crushed red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup pesto sauce
  • 1/3 cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, drained and cut into strips

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute garlic until tender, then stir in chicken and season with red pepper flakes. Cook until chicken is golden and cooked through.
  3. Combine pasta, chicken, pesto, and sun-dried tomatoes in a large bowl; toss to coat evenly.
